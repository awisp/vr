Virtual Reality in Outer Space

Official website for 2018 eighth grade robotics team of Weston, Massachusetts. We have designed VR scenes that each have a corresponding scent that will trigger memories. Many astronauts who travel to space for long periods of time become lonely or homesick, and we hope to use smells and pictures to comfort them. With plenty of research, our team has been able to create VR images that are compatible for phones, computers, and headsets. Please check the VR Demos page for the demonstrations.
