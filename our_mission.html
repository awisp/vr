VR in Outer Space

Helping astronauts experience home.

Our Mission

Astronauts in space often have to deal with long periods of time away from family, friends, and earth. Many are lonely and homesick which can cause both mental and physical problems. They also tackle stress frequently due to lack of variety and confined space. With this, sprang our idea of VR, smells, and sounds. Because smell is our strongest sense, we decided to use different smells that correspond with each scene. In addition, we added calming music and sounds on loop.
